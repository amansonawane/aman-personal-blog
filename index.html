about me

curious. driven. hungry. on a quest to be better.

there are more than 1,130,000,000 websites on this planet. thanks for visiting mine. hi. my name is aman sonawane. i am currently working as a private equity analyst at succession capital partners india, a self-funded search fund that is looking to acquire a traditional indian family business. we are one of the pioneers of the entrepreneurship through acquisition (eta) model in india which was originally conceived at the stanford graduate school of business in 1984.

i graduated from scac, pune and i’m most excited about startups, tech, vc, pe, video games, comic book media, motorcycling and more. i will mostly post opinionated and informative blogs here on basically any topic that strikes my mood. i’m an ai optimist who believes that ai will significantly improve the quality of life for all humans. i aspire to walk to down the path of entrepreneurship myself one day and build for the greater good.

p.s. we are looking to acquire a traditional indian family business! know someone who might be interested? refer them to me at aman@successioncapital.in. we will provide a handsome commission if we successfully close the deal.
